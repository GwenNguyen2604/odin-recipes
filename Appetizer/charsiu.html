Air Fryer Char Siu

Ingredients

  • 3 lb thick cut pork chop
  • 8 tbsp honey
  • 8 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 4 tbsp soy sauce
  • 8 cloves garlic smashed
  • 4-inch ginger sliced
  • 8 tsp Shaoxing wine (or sherry - optional)
  • 2 tsp Chinese five spice powder
  • 1 tsp ground white pepper (optional)

Directions

  1. Cut the pork chop into 1" pieces. Combine the remaining ingredients into a marinade, then marinate the pork in the fridge. Longer is better, ideally go at least 30 minutes, but overnight is best.
  2. Place the pork into the air fryer basket with at least 1/4" gap between pieces, in a single layer. Set the air fryer to 375ÂºF for 8 minutes.10 minutes if more well-done pork is preferred.
  3. On a saucepan, bring the marinade juice to a boil for 1-2 minutes to use as a sauce or glaze for an extra rich char siu.
  4. Enjoy with white rice or on its own as a snack.
