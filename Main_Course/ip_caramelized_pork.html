Instant Pot Caramelized Pork

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds pork butt (shoulder) cut in 1-2" pieces
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • ¼ cup fish sauce
  • 4 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small stalk of fresh ginger, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup coconut water
  • ½ cup water
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • Salt and pepper
  • Green onions or cilantro for garnish

Directions

  1. Season the pork cubes generously with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the cooking oil in the Instant Pot on the "normal" sauté setting.
  3. Add the pork (in 2-3 batches) and brown on all sides. Be sure not to crowd the pork or it will not properly brown.
  4. Place all the browned pork in the Instant Pot and add the sugar. Cook until the pork turns a golden-brown color (about 5 minutes). Stir constantly to keep sugar from burning.
  5. Add the garlic and ginger and sauté for 1 minute.
  6. Add the remaining ingredients (except sliced onions) and set the Instant Pot to pressure cook on high for 15 minutes. Let the steam release naturally for 10 minutes. Use the quick release to release any remaining pressure.
  7. Turn the Instant Pot to sauté and add the onions. Simmer until sauce has reduced to a thickened glaze, about 5-10 minutes.
  8. Remove from heat, garnish with green onions or cilantro, and serve with rice.
