Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 ½ lb lean ground beef
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 1/2 large bell pepper (any color)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 28-oz can tomato sauce
  • 3 ounces (half a 6-oz can) tomato paste
  • 1 14-oz can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 tbsp dried parsley
  • 1 tbsp Italian Seasoning
  • Pinch of garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp to 1/4 cup sugar (optional)
  • Salt

    • For the lasagna assembly:

  • 1/2 lb dry lasagna noodles (about 9)
  • 15 ounces Ricotta cheese
  • 24 oz Mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 4 oz freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Cook lasagna noodle according to package instruction.
  2. In a medium sized pot heat 2 teaspoons of olive oil on medium high heat. Add the ground beef and cook until it is lightly browned on all sides. Remove the beef to a bowl. Drain off all but a tablespoon of fat.
  3. Add the diced bell pepper and onions to the pot, cook for 4 to 5 minutes, until softened. Add the minced garlic and cook half a minute more. Return the browned ground beef to the pot. Stir to combine, reduce the heat to low and cook for another 5 minutes.
  4. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste to the pot. Then add the rest of the spices, adjusting the amounts to taste.
  5. Add red or white wine vinegar. Stir in sugar, a tablespoon at a time, tasting after each addition. Add salt, note that you will later be adding Parmesan, which is salty.
  6. Bring the sauce to a simmer and then lower the heat to maintain a low simmer. Cook for 15 to 45 minutes, stirring often. Remove from heat once done.
  7. Once the noodle is done, drain in a colander and rinse with cool water, gently separating any noodles that may be sticking together. Spread a little olive oil on a large baking sheet, and lay out the cooked noodles on this sheet, turning them over so that they get coated with a little of the olive oil. This will prevent them from sticking together.
  8. In a 9x13-inch casserole dish, ladle a cup of sauce and spread it over the bottom of the dish. Arrange one layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce. Ladle 1/3 of the remaining sauce over the noodles. Sprinkle 1/3 of the grated mozzarella on top of lasagna sauce. Add half of the ricotta cheese in dollops. Sprinkle half the parmesan cheese evenly over the top of ricotta cheese.
  9. Apply second layer of noodles, top it with half of the remaining sauce. Add half of the remaining Mozzarella, the remaining ricotta cheese, and another the remaining Parmesan.
  10. Finish with another layer of noodles. Spread the remaining sauce over the top layer of noodles and sprinkle with the remaining Mozzarella cheese.
  11. Cover the lasagna pan with aluminum foil. Bake at 375°F for 45 minutes. Uncover in the last 10 for a crusty top or edges. Let cool for at least 15 minutes before serving.
